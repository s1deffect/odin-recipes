Lasagna

Description

Lasagna originated in Italy during the Middle Ages. The oldest transcribed text about lasagna appears in 1282 in the Memoriali Bolognesi ("Bolognesi Memorials")

Ingredients

  • onin
  • tomato
  • sugar
  • spices
  • cheeses

    • Steps

    1. Make the meat sauce.
    2. Cook the noodles.
    3. Make the ricotta mixture.
    4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
    5. Cover with foil and bake.
    6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
